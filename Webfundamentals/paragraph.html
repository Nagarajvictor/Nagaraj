Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is the current captain of the India national team. He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League as the right-handed batsman. He is widely considered one of the world's best batsmen.

Kohli captained India Under-19s to victory at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup in Malaysia. A few months later, he made his ODI debut for India against Sri Lanka at the age of 19.
